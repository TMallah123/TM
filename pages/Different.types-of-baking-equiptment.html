Different types of baking Equiptment

Equiptment use necessity
Stand Mixer A stand mixer is used to wisk and mix ingredients together. It is fast and efficient but you want to make sure you don't over mix certain types of batter. A stand mixer is easy to use and if you have one can be a very useful. A stand mixer is not necessay but is very nice to have. Most things can be mixed by hand but may take more time or work. For example when making meringues your egg whites need to be wisked a lot. This task can be done by hand but when using a mixer it is a lot easier and faster.
Silicon Mat A silicon mat is a very nice tool to use. it can be used instead of parchment or wax paper for a baking pan. A silicon mat also elimates the use of cooking spray. I use my silicon mat for everything and it is one of my favorite types of baking equiptment. I think that a silicon mat is necessary because it can be used over and over again and is not wasteful. Wax paper can be used instead of a silicon mat but overall a silicon mat is better.
Measuring cups Measuring cups ensure accuracy. They are easy to use and can be used for everything. being acurate in the amount of ingredients is very important Measuring cups are definetly neccesary for the outcome of what you are making
Spatulas A spatula is a hand-held tool that is used for lifting, flipping, folding or spreading. A spatula is essential for many types of cooking and baking. They are especially needed when frying foods that need to be flipped or sticky batters that need to come out of the bowl. Spatulas make sure that no batter is left in the bowl. Spatulas are neccessary for baking and are defenitly extremly helpful. they help with not wasting food and help for keeping your hands clean.
DIfferent types of pans and trays There are cake pans, pie pans cookie sheets, muffin tins, and many more types of trays and pans. They are all used to bake or set a desert and are all used in there own way. It is neccessary to have some kind of tray or pan but there are many different sizes and shapes that are fun but not as necessary. For certain types deserts you may need a different pan or tray but they are easy to get buy.
